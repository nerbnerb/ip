# Date/time syntax

You can omit the year to default to the current year, and the space between the time and AM/PM.
For example, to key in the date **23 August 2022**, time **6:00pm**, you can use:

- `d MMM yyyy h:mm a`
    - 23 Aug 2022 6:00 pm
- `d MMM yyyy h a`
    - 23 Aug 2022 6pm
- `d MMM h:mm a`
    - 23 Aug 6:00pm
- `d MMM h a`
    - 23 Aug 6 pm
- `d/M/yyyy HHmm`
    - 23/8/2022 1800
- `d/M HHmm`
    - 23/8 1800
